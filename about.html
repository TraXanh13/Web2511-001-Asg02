Founded in 1969, in the heart of Calgary, Alberta, Blossom has been serving locals, and tourisit alike for 50 years. At blossom,we believe that food should be represented like the way of the samurai, with precision and grace. With a comforting taste similar to the cherry blossoms.

Our number one goal is to satisfy the cravings of individuals for food no matter their mood, while keeping it delicious, nutricious and healthy.

The food we serve has been perfected for years, giving us the confidence to ensure that every plate leaving the kitchen is the best our chefs can serve.

Our food is deeply embeded with tradition that we believe in the old saying of the samurai to "Measure twice, cut once".
